👩‍💼 About Me

Welcome! I am Prof. Asha Ayakar, a passionate educator and researcher in the domain of Electronics & Communication Engineering. With years of dedicated academic experience, I strive to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, nurturing engineering students to become innovative problem-solvers.

My primary areas of expertise include Digital Logic Design, VLSI Design, and Embedded Systems. I am committed to quality education, continuous research, and mentoring the next generation of engineers.
